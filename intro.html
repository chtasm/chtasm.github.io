Introduction

The Suzuki SV650 is a 645cc V-Twin[1] motorcycle produced by the Suzuki Motor Corporation from 1999 to 2009 and then again for the upcoming 2017 model year until at least 2023. The motorcycle comes in two flavors: a classic looking "N" naked model calling back to motorcycles of old that came without fairings or cowlings, and a more modern styled, fully faired "S" type with a more aggressive look and riding position.

The SV650 is seen as a beginner to intermediate/advanced level bike as it has a very consistent and forgiving throttle that allows new riders to grow accustomed to controlling their acceleration, but also includes a quite powerful engine that begets very exciting top speeds that will surely satisfy many motorcycle veterans and bored newbies.

The bike now is relatively cheap, still very reliable, parts are readily available and maintenance is affordable. Riding-wise it's big enough for medium to tallish males, but small enough for smaller females to ride. Their handling is great for urban commuting, and they have plenty of power for solo highway touring.
